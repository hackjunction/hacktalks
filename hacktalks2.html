Hack\Talks this year

KEYNOTES

Insightful and inspiring keynotes from technical experts in security, web, cloud and data science. Five future-looking keynotes for the largest Hack Talks audience to date will give you something to think and talk about.

WORKSHOPS

Two tracks of workshops from a variety of industry-leaders are a great opportunity to deep dive into current topics. Hands-on sessions provide immediately actionable takeaways for professionals and students alike.
